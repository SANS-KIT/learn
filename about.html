Team.

SANSkit is maintained by a Small team coding noobs currently studying in IIT JAMMU Btech 2020 UG batch.



Goals.

• To help the student read real Sanskrit as quickly as possible

• To simplify the process of learning Sanskrit without simplifying the language itself

• To present Sanskrit grammar clearly, legibly, and beautifully

• To create a free guide that can rival any Sanskrit textbook in depth and quality

• To popularize the study of Indian classical languages



Mission.

Our mission is to make learning Sanskrit to society tunneled through technology. We also strive to make learning Sanskrit portable, convenient and equip it with the latest technology. We wish to make it student centric and mould them to be more independent.
